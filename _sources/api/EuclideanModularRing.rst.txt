EuclideanModularRing(S, R, Mod, reduction, merge, exactQuo)
===========================================================

:viewcode:`modring.spad line 87 <modring.spad#L87>` :editcode:`[edit on github] <modring.spad#L87>`

* S: \ :ref:`CommutativeRing <l436f6d6d7574617469766552696e67>`\ 

* R: \ :ref:`UnivariatePolynomialCategory <l556e6976617269617465506f6c796e6f6d69616c43617465676f7279>`\  S
