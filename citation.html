Citation¶

How to cite FriCAS¶

If you use FriCAS in your research, you should use the following BibTeX entry to refer to FriCAS in any publication.

@Misc{FriCAS,
  key =          {FriCAS},
  author =       {{FriCAS team}},
  year =         {2025},
  title =        {{FriCAS} 1.3.12---an advanced computer algebra system},
  note =         {Available at \url{http://fricas.github.io}}
}
