FreeMagma VarSet¶

xlpoly.spad line 1

  • VarSet: OrderedSet

This type is the basic representation of parenthesized words (binary trees over arbitrary symbols) useful in LiePolynomial. Author: Michel Petitot (petitot@lifl.fr).
