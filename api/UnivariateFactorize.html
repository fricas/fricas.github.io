  • ZP: UnivariatePolynomialCategory Integer

Package for the factorization of univariate polynomials with integer coefficients. The factorization is done by “lifting” (HENSEL) the factorization over a finite field.

factor: ZP -> Factored ZP
factor(m) returns the factorization of m
factorSquareFree: ZP -> Factored ZP
factorSquareFree(m) returns the factorization of m square free polynomial
henselFact: (ZP, Boolean) -> Record(contp: Integer, factors: List Record(irr: ZP, pow: Integer))
henselFact(m, flag) returns the factorization of m, FinalFact is a Record s.t. FinalFact.contp=content m, FinalFact.factors=List of irreducible factors of m with exponent , if flag =true the polynomial is assumed square free.
