Various popular distributions.

arcsineDistribution: R -> Distribution R if R has Algebra Fraction Integer

arcsineDistribution(a) produces the arcsine distribution of variance a.

bernoulliDistribution01: R -> Distribution R

bernoulliDistribution01(a) produces the distribution of a projection of trace a

freePoissonDistribution: R -> Distribution R

freePoissonDistribution(a) produces a free Poisson distribution of variance a.

gaussianDistribution: R -> Distribution R

gaussianDistribution(a) produces a gaussian distribution of variance a.

poissonDistribution: R -> Distribution R

poissonDistribution(a) produces a Poisson distribution of variance a.

wignerDistribution: R -> Distribution R

wignerDistribution(a) produces a Wigner distribution of variance a.
