Dense Puiseux series in one variable UnivariatePuiseuxSeries is a domain representing Puiseux series in one variable with coefficients in an arbitrary ring. The parameters of the type specify the coefficient ring, the power series variable, and the center of the power series expansion. For example, UnivariatePuiseuxSeries(Integer, x, 3) represents Puiseux series in (x - 3) with Integer coefficients.
